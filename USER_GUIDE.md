## ğŸ“ Adding Items

### Step-by-Step:

1. **Enter Item Name**
   - Type the name of the item you want to buy
   - Example: "Milk", "Bread", "Apples"
   - Must be at least 2 characters
   - Maximum 50 characters

2. **Set Quantity**
   - Use the **-** button to decrease
   - Use the **+** button to increase
   - Or type a number directly (1-9999)
   - Default is 1

3. **Add to List**
   - Click the green **"Add Item"** button
   - You'll see a success message
   - The item appears at the top of your list
   - The form clears, ready for the next item

### Tips:
- âœ“ Be specific with item names (e.g., "2% Milk" instead of just "Milk")
- âœ“ Set the correct quantity to avoid multiple trips

## âœï¸ Editing Items

### How to Edit:

1. **Start Editing**
   - Find the item you want to edit
   - Click the blue **pencil icon** on the right
   - The form changes to "Edit Mode"

2. **Make Changes**
   - The item's current name and quantity appear in the form
   - Modify the name and/or quantity
   - Changes aren't saved until you click "Save"

3. **Save or Cancel**
   - Click green **"Save"** button to save changes
   - Click gray **"Cancel"** button to discard changes
   - You'll see a success message when saved

### When to Edit:
- Correct spelling mistakes
- Change quantity after counting what you need
- Update item details without deleting and re-adding
